Frequently Asked Questions

Will this guarantee ISO 9001 certification?

No. ISO 9001 certification depends on implementation, leadership commitment, records and audit performance. Documentation alone cannot guarantee certification.

Do you provide consulting or implementation support?

No. QMS Blueprint provides documentation only. Consulting, training, implementation and auditing services are intentionally excluded.

Are these documents acceptable to certification bodies?

The documentation is structured to align with ISO 9001 requirements, but acceptance depends on how the system is implemented and how effectively it is demonstrated during audits.

Can we modify the documents internally?

Yes. All documents are delivered in editable Microsoft Word and Excel formats and are intended to be customised to reflect real processes.

Do you review our implementation or readiness?

No. QMS Blueprint does not perform audits, gap assessments or pre‑certification reviews.

Is this suitable for small businesses?

Yes, provided the organisation is willing to implement and maintain the system proportionally and honestly.
